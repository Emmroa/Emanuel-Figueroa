Mātauranga Nova

An AI chat companion paired with an operator analytics dashboard — science-led, culturally safe, sovereign NZ AI. Listens without judgment, informs without fear, and never stores users’ words.

A privacy-first AI chat platform for HIV & sexual-health communities in Aotearoa, built solo end-to-end as a prototype for a national health innovation challenge. Full-stack React & Node, local LLM, conversational interface with crisis pathways, live data from public Health NZ reports, zero-data-retention architecture aligned with the NZ Privacy Act 2020, IPP 3A, HIPC 2020 and Te Mana Raraunga.
